About Smart Agro Uganda

Bridging tradition with technology for Ugandan farmers

Our Mission

We are a Ugandan‑led initiative helping smallholder farmers access modern markets, practical skills, and affordable smart machinery. Operating in all regions – from Mbale to Mbarara, Gulu to Jinja.

  • 💧 Solar water pumps & smart sprinklers at cooperative prices
